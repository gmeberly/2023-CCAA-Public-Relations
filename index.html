Welcome to the very first Online National Silent Auction offered by the Cane Corso Association of America!

The rules are simple. The Online Silent Auction will start on Tuesday, July 18th, 2023, at 9:00 AM CST and close on Saturday, July 22nd, 2023, at 4:00 PM CST. During that time, members are encouraged to fiercely bid on the listed item(s) in whole dollar increments to remain the top bidder or purchase the basket outright. The choice is yours!

All bids and sales of the Online Silent Auction items are final. The winner will be contacted via the email supplied at bidding and agrees to pay the bid amount within 24 hours after being notified or will forfeit their winnings, unless optioned for Buy Out. Detailed instructions on finalizing payment will be sent when contacted. Shipping costs will be covered by the winner and items will be sold as is.

Each bidder assumes all risks and hazards related to the silent auction and items. The Cane Corso Association of America (CCAA) is not liable for any damage in the shipping process, or usage of items. All items that will be shipped will be securely packed. If a bidder wins and will be at the National Event, they may pick up their winnings in lieu of shipment. Enjoy reviewing all the amazing sponsorship donations for our exciting event!
